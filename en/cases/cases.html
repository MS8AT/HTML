How to choose faster

  • Mini-ITX — for compact builds when desk space is limited.
  • ATX — a versatile format with good airflow and room for liquid cooling and multiple drives.
  • Tempered glass gives the build a more premium look, while matte SPCC looks cleaner and more understated.
  • Rack — for server and industrial rack installations.

What is in the catalog

Most models are full-size ATX cases in black and white with a transparent side panel. Rack cases for server solutions and one compact Mini-ITX option are also available.
